11.3.69 enable_breakpoints/1   development

Synopsis

enable_breakpoints(+BIDs)

Enables the breakpoints specified by BIDs.

Arguments

BIDs

list of integer, must be ground

Breakpoint identifiers.

Exceptions

instantiation_error
type_error

in BIDs
